Our Story

Our journey began in 2021 when we met on Discord. I was immediately captivated by your voice and your way of talking. Intrigued, I started following you on Instagram and gradually built up the courage to start a conversation.

As we talked more, my feelings grew stronger. Eventually, I confessed my feelings, and to my joy, you felt the same way. On March 30, 2022, we officially started dating, beginning our adventure in a long-distance relationship.

For nearly a year, we nurtured our love across the distance, until February 11, 2023, when we finally met in person in Marrakesh. I stayed for three unforgettable days, solidifying our bond in the beautiful city.

Since then, we've continued our long-distance relationship, cherishing every moment we get to spend together in person. Despite the challenges of distance, our love has grown stronger, proving that true connection knows no boundaries.

Now, on our third anniversary, I want to celebrate the amazing journey we've shared and look forward to all the beautiful moments yet to come. Thank you for being the most wonderful partner I could ever ask for.
